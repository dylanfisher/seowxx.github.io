TEMPO 
 is a project that translates the tempo of Korean poem into English by using scrolls on webpages. 
 The spacing between words/phrases of the poem is set in certain tempo to pace your reading. 
 The poem you will be reading is "Cottony Snowflakes" by a Korean poet Choi, Do Sun 
 Here is the original text of Cottony Snowflakes.
